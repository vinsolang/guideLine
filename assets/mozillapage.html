To Install ThunderBird on your computer

1. Visit this ThunderBird download page in any browser,such as Microsoft Edge

2. Click the button. The ThunderBird Installer that downloads will automatically offer you the best
available version of ThunderBird for your Computer.

3. Click Open file in the Downloads notification on Microsoft Edge to start the process.

4. In other browsers, you may need to first save the ThunderBird installer to your computer, then open the file you
downloaded and click next button.

5. Click choose Standard and click next.

6. And than click install this program

7. And click finish

8. When u in Thunderbird u should Set up Your Existing Email Address.
Your username ,Email ,and Password.

9. And click Done button.

10. And than click Set Default button. Your install and set up finished. . .
